FestPix: How It Works Photographer Setup Guide

What You Need

  1. FestPix.com Subscription
  2. Smartphone or Tablet
    • We recommend using a device enabled with at least 3G data but if you have access to a wifi connection that will work as well.
    • We recommended bringing the device charger ot portable power pack along with the device.
  3. Digital camera enabled with wifi transfer technology.
    • We recommend Eyefi (www.eyefi.com) but any type of transfer will work.
    • (Find out if your camera is compatible with Eyefi Here)

Step 1: Account Creation

Create an account at FestPix.com.

Step 2: Customize Your Settings

Modify your settings to fit your personal brand. With Festpix you can change:

  • The text message copy received by the person you're delivering the photos to.
  • Photo watermark
  • Photo view gallery logo
  • Photo view gallery colorway
  • Social share options
  • Custom button link

This can be done via your settings panel.

Step 3: Setup Your Device

Connect your smartphone or tablet to the Eyefi card or other transfer technology.

If you're using EyeFi, help can be found here: http://www.eyefi.com/support


To Save Time:

We recommend saving the FestPix web app to your home screen (iOS). To do this:

  • Go to New Photo Session on your iOS device and press the 'share' button.
  • Press "Add to Home Screen".
  • This page will now appear as an app on your device's home screen.

Arranging the FestPix, EyeFi, and your settings app together will allow you to create and upload sessions much faster.

Step 4: Create Photo Sessions

Take pictures of an individual or group and transfer them via eyefi from your camera to your phone. The process goes in this order:

  1. Take photos with camera

  2. Transfer photos to device

    • You'll need EyeFi or an alternate wifi transfer technology for this. Once the photos you want to send are stored on your device, you may continue to the next step.

  3. Upload & tag photos with FestPix

    • If you haven't already, open the New Photo Session page on your device.
    • Here, enter the phone numbers and/or email addresses you'd like your photos to be delivered to.
    • Click the photo icons to select the photos you wish to send. These will all be sent in one link.
    • Select the event associated with the photo session. This will keep your photo sessions organized.
    • Press Create Photo Session
    • Your photos will now upload to FestPix servers, where they are instantly delivered to the phone numbers and/or emails inputted with the session.
